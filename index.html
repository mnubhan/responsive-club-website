Every Child Deserves a Fair Chance to Learn and Thrive

Teach For The Needs supports underprivileged children with emotional, cognitive, and academic care—after school ends.Together, we nurture minds and open doors to brighter futures.

About Us

Background

A non-profit organisation, Teach for the Needs strives to raise the status of underprivileged children and orphans by meeting their basic needs; education on par with others.

Objective

Provide equal exposure in terms of education, emotion and cognitive support in children's development after normal school hours.

IT Workshop With Students

Our IT workshops introduce students to the world of technology through hands-on learning. From basic computer skills to creative coding exercises, these sessions build digital confidence and spark curiosity in young minds.

Educational Discussion Through Social Media

We use social media as a platform for educational engagement—sharing knowledge, starting meaningful discussions, and connecting students with mentors and peers beyond the classroom walls in a safe, guided space.

Provide Online Learning Essentials

To ensure no child is left behind, we equip underprivileged students with the tools they need for remote education—laptops, internet access, headphones, and other essential supplies for successful online learning.

Online Class With Our Tutors

Our experienced tutors offer structured, interactive online classes tailored to each child’s needs. These sessions provide academic support, boost confidence, and help students stay on track in their learning journey.
